Privacy Request

You can use this page to exercise your privacy rights regarding the personal information Obliger holds about you.

Delete Your Account

To delete your account and all associated data, go to Settings in the Obliger app and select Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Other Requests

To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by another method, or to ask any other privacy-related question, email us at contact@obligerapp.com. We will respond within 30 days.
